HOME

For over three decades, we at Lohner Family Shoes have been bringing our community custom, handmade, high quality shoes. From women's dress shoes, to men's work boots, our cobbler's can tackle any footwear needs. Find out how to get your own pair at our product listings.

Even before Lohner Family Shoes opened in 1989, old man “Scruffy” Lohner was cobbling in his own village, deep in the California hills during the gold rush in the 1800s!
